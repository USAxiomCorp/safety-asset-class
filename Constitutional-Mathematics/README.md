# Constitutional Mathematics — Overview
## Foundation for Aligned Intelligence, Truth, and Humanity (FAITH)

Constitutional Mathematics is the formal mathematical substrate underlying the Safety Asset Class (SAC), Russell Recursive Refinement (R³), RUAX operator families, RAC audit chain, and CSL hardware gate. It defines the fixed‑point constraints, arithmetic rules, and constitutional boundaries that ensure deterministic, machine‑verifiable safety in autonomous and robotic systems.

This directory contains the axioms, arithmetic structures, proofs, and constraint definitions that form the foundation of the standard.

---

## 1. Purpose
Constitutional Mathematics provides the mathematical guarantees required for:
- deterministic safety boundaries,
- fixed‑point constraint enforcement,
- refinement‑based evaluation (R³),
- operator correctness (RUAX),
- immutable auditability (RAC),
- hardware‑level constraint enforcement (CSL).

It replaces probabilistic safety models with **constitutional, enforceable, mathematically grounded rules**.
