# RUAX Execution Model — Glossary
## Foundation for Aligned Intelligence, Truth, and Humanity (FAITH)

This glossary defines the formal terminology used throughout the RUAX Execution Model.  
All definitions are normative and required for SAC certification, R³ refinement, MVL legality, RAC auditability, and CSL enforcement.

---

## A

### **Arithmetic Error Bound**
The maximum allowable numerical error under WAD arithmetic.

### **Audit Trace**
A RAC‑anchored record of operator execution.

---

## B

### **Boundary Condition**
A constitutional or legal limit that execution must not cross.

---

## C

### **CSL Verification**
Hardware‑level confirmation that execution adhered to constitutional and legal constraints.

### **Commit**
A deterministic execution primitive that finalizes a state transition.

### **Constraint Preservation**
The requirement that execution must not alter the truth value of constitutional constraints.

---

## D

### **Deterministic Execution**
The property that identical inputs must always produce identical outputs.

### **Deterministic Replay**
The ability to reproduce execution exactly from RUAX traces.

---

## E

### **Execution Context**
The domain in which an operator executes (RUAX, R³, MVL, RAC, CSL).

### **Execution Path**
The ordered sequence of operator steps taken during execution.

---

## F

### **Fixed‑Point Operator (FPO)**
An operator that computes or approximates constitutional fixed‑points.

---

## H

### **Hardware Boundary Event**
A CSL‑generated event triggered when execution approaches or attempts to cross a boundary.

---

## L

### **Legal Evaluation**
The deterministic evaluation of MVL constraints during execution.

---

## O

### **Operator**
A deterministic RUAX function that transforms system state.

### **Operator Parameters**
The explicit inputs to a RUAX operator.

---

## P

### **Projection Operator (PO)**
An operator that projects states back into the constitutional boundary.

---

## R

### **RAC Anchor**
A cryptographic hash linking a RUAX trace to a RAC block.

### **Rollback**
A deterministic execution primitive that reverts to a previous state.

### **RUAX**
Refinement‑Unified Axiomatic Execution — the deterministic execution model of SAC.

---

## S

### **State Hash**
A cryptographic hash representing the system state before or after execution.

### **State‑Update Operator (SUO)**
An operator that updates system state deterministically.

---

## T

### **Trace Entry**
A single RUAX trace record containing header, body, and footer.

### **TraceID**
A unique identifier for a RUAX trace entry.

---
