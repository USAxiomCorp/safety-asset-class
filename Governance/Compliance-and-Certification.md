# Compliance and Certification Policy
## Foundation for Aligned Intelligence, Truth, and Humanity (FAITH)

This document defines the requirements, processes, and verification pathways for achieving compliance with the Safety Asset Class (SAC), Constitutional Mathematics, Russell Recursive Refinement (R³), RUAX, RAC, CSL, and the broader machine‑verifiable law ecosystem.

Compliance ensures that autonomous and robotic systems operate within deterministic, mathematically enforceable safety boundaries.

---

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of SAC compliance is to guarantee that any system claiming adherence to the standard:
- satisfies constitutional constraints,
- operates within bounded error,
- supports machine‑verifiable auditability,
- implements deterministic safety mechanisms,
- and maintains alignment with the mathematical substrate.

Certification is the formal recognition that a system meets these requirements.

---

## 2. Compliance Requirements

### 2.1 Constitutional Mathematics
A compliant system must:
- implement WAD arithmetic where required,
- enforce constitutional constraints at runtime,
- maintain fixed‑point consistency across all safety‑critical operations.

### 2.2 Russell Recursive Refinement (R³)
A compliant system must:
- support refinement‑based safety evaluation,
- expose refinement layers for audit,
- maintain monotonic safety improvement under R³ operators.

### 2.3 RUAX Execution Model
A compliant system must:
- implement RUAX operator families correctly,
- maintain deterministic operator behavior,
- expose operator traces for RAC audit.

### 2.4 RAC Audit Chain
A compliant system must:
- generate RAC‑compatible audit logs,
- support cryptographic anchoring of safety events,
- maintain immutable auditability of constraint checks.

### 2.5 CSL Hardware Gate
A compliant system must:
- implement CSL hardware or equivalent verifiable logic,
- enforce constitutional constraints at the hardware boundary,
- support simulation‑based verification.

- R³ refinement exposure

### Level 3 — Full SAC Compliance
