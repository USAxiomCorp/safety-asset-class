# Governance Overview
## Foundation for Aligned Intelligence, Truth, and Humanity (FAITH)

The Governance directory contains the constitutional, procedural, and operational documents that define how the Safety Asset Class (SAC), Constitutional Mathematics, Russell Recursive Refinement (R³), RUAX, RAC, CSL, and related standards are maintained, updated, and stewarded.

These documents establish FAITH as the standards authority and outline the processes that ensure mathematical integrity, deterministic safety, and long‑term institutional trust.

## Contents

- **Foundation-Charter.md**  
  The constitutional document defining FAITH’s authority, mission, and governance structure.

- **Versioning-Policy.md**  
  The formal rules for how SAC and related standards evolve through MAJOR.MINOR.CONSTRAINT releases.

- **CONTRIBUTING.md**  
  The process for proposing changes, submitting PRs, and participating in the standards ecosystem.

- **Compliance-and-Certification.md** *(added when ready)*  
  Requirements for SAC certification, RAC audit validation, and CSL hardware verification.

- **Code-of-Conduct.md** *(optional)*  
  Behavioral expectations for contributors and participants.

## Purpose

The Governance directory ensures that:
- the standard evolves predictably and transparently,
- mathematical and constitutional integrity are preserved,
- contributions follow a rigorous review process,
- institutions can trust the stability and authority of the framework.

## Authority

All governance documents are maintained by FAITH and may be amended only through the formal processes defined in the Foundation Charter.
