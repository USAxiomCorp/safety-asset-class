# Code of Conduct
## Foundation for Aligned Intelligence, Truth, and Humanity (FAITH)

This Code of Conduct establishes the behavioral expectations for all contributors, reviewers, implementers, and participants engaging with the Safety Asset Class (SAC), Constitutional Mathematics, Russell Recursive Refinement (R³), RUAX, RAC, CSL, and related standards.

FAITH maintains a professional, rigorous, and mathematically grounded environment. All participants are expected to uphold these principles.

---

## 1. Professionalism
Participants must:
- communicate clearly and respectfully,
- focus on technical, mathematical, or architectural substance,
- avoid personal attacks, harassment, or disruptive behavior,
- maintain integrity in all contributions.

---

## 2. Technical Rigor
Contributions must:
- be grounded in logic, mathematics, or verifiable engineering,
- avoid speculation without justification,
- respect the constitutional and deterministic foundations of the standard.

Unsupported claims, vague proposals, or non‑rigorous arguments may be rejected.

---

## 3. Respect for the Standard
Participants must:
- avoid misrepresenting SAC or related frameworks,
- avoid claiming compliance without certification,
- avoid introducing changes that weaken safety, determinism, or auditability.

---

## 4. Collaboration
Participants are encouraged to:
- provide constructive feedback,
- help clarify specifications,
- assist others in understanding mathematical or architectural concepts,
- maintain a cooperative and solution‑oriented mindset.

---

## 5. Zero Tolerance for Abuse
FAITH does not permit:
- harassment,
- discrimination,
- threats,
- hostile behavior,
- attempts to undermine the governance process.

Violations may result in removal from discussions or revocation of participation privileges.

---

## 6. Enforcement
The Standards Steward and Governance Group may:
- issue warnings,
- restrict participation,
- reject contributions,
- revoke access,
- escalate issues as needed.

Decisions are final and guided by the Foundation Charter.

---

## 7. Scope
This Code applies to:
- GitHub repositories,
- discussions,
- proposals,
- reviews,
- meetings,
- all official FAITH communication channels.

---

## 8. Mission Alignment
All participants must act in alignment with FAITH’s mission:
to maintain a constitutional, mathematically grounded foundation for deterministic safety in autonomous and robotic systems.
