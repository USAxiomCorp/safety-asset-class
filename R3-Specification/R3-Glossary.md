# Russell Recursive Refinement (R³) — Glossary
## Foundation for Aligned Intelligence, Truth, and Humanity (FAITH)

This glossary defines the formal terminology used throughout the R³ specification.  
All definitions are normative and required for SAC certification, RUAX execution, MVL legality, RAC auditability, and CSL enforcement.

---

## A

### **Attraction Operator (FPAO)**
A refinement operator that pulls system states toward constitutional fixed‑points.

---

## B

### **Boundary Constraint**
A constitutional limit that refinement cannot cross.

---

## C

### **Composite Refinement Operator**
The ordered composition of all R³ operator families:
GARO → CPO → FPAO → RNO.

### **Constraint Invariance**
The requirement that refinement must not alter the truth value of any constitutional constraint.

---

## D

### **Deterministic Refinement**
The property that refinement produces identical outputs for identical inputs.

---

## F

### **Fixed‑Point**
A state \( S^* \) such that:
\[
R(S^*) = S^*
\]

### **Fixed‑Point Stability**
The requirement that refinement converges toward fixed‑points under repeated application.

---

## G

### **GARO (Gradient‑Aligned Refinement Operator)**
An operator that reduces constitutional risk by following the gradient of the risk functional.

---

## M

### **Monotonic Refinement**
The requirement that constitutional risk must never increase during refinement.

---

## P

### **Projection Operator (CPO)**
An operator that projects states back into the constitutional boundary.

---

## R

### **R³ (Russell Recursive Refinement)**
The refinement framework that ensures monotonic safety improvement and convergence to constitutional fixed‑points.

### **RAC‑Anchored Refinement**
Refinement steps recorded immutably in the RAC audit chain.

### **Refinement Sequence**
A sequence of states:
\[
S_0, S_1, S_2, \ldots
\]
generated by repeated application of the composite refinement operator.

### **Risk Functional**
A constitutional function \( \mathcal{R}(S) \) that measures system risk.

### **Risk‑Neutralization Operator (RNO)**
An operator that removes specific components of constitutional risk.

---

## S

### **State**
A complete representation of the system at a given refinement step.

---

## T

### **Termination Condition**
The condition:
\[
S_{n+1} = S_n
\]
indicating convergence to a fixed‑point.
