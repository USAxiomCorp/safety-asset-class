# Safety Asset Class (SAC) — Glossary
## Foundation for Aligned Intelligence, Truth, and Humanity (FAITH)

This glossary defines the formal terminology used throughout the Safety Asset Class (SAC).  
All definitions are normative and required for SAC certification, refinement safety, legal compliance, deterministic execution, immutable auditability, and hardware‑level enforcement.

---

## A

### **Aligned Intelligence**
An intelligent system whose behavior is constrained by SAC, MVL, RUAX, RAC, and CSL.

### **Audit Integrity**
The guarantee that all traces and blocks are tamper‑evident and immutable.

### **Audit Layer**
The RAC subsystem responsible for immutable auditability and deterministic replay.

---

## B

### **Boundary Condition**
A constitutional or legal limit that must not be crossed.

### **Boundary Event**
A RAC‑anchored or CSL‑generated event triggered when a boundary is approached or violated.

---

## C

### **CSL (Constitutional Safety Layer)**
The Tier 3 hardware enforcement layer that makes violations physically impossible.

### **Constraint Preservation**
The requirement that constitutional constraints remain invariant across refinement and execution.

### **Certification Tier**
One of the three SAC certification levels: T1, T2, T3.

---

## D

### **Deterministic Execution**
The property that identical inputs must always produce identical outputs.

### **Deterministic Replay**
The ability to reproduce system behavior exactly from RAC traces.

---

## E

### **Enforcement Layer**
One of the five SAC enforcement layers: R³, MVL, RUAX, RAC, CSL.

### **Escalation**
A mandatory halt triggered when uncertainty exceeds the maximum allowable threshold.

---

## F

### **FAITH**
The governing authority responsible for maintaining SAC and the entire constitutional safety stack.

### **Fixed‑Point**
A refinement state that satisfies all constitutional and legal constraints.

---

## H

### **Hardware Enforcement**
Physical enforcement of constraints, legality, and boundaries by CSL.

---

## L

### **Legal Constraint**
A rule defined by MVL that must be satisfied by all system states.

### **Legal Replayability**
The ability to reproduce legal decisions exactly from RAC traces.

---

## M

### **MVL (Machine‑Verifiable Law)**
The legal subsystem of SAC that defines deterministic, machine‑verifiable legal constraints.

### **Merkle Root**
The root hash of the Merkle tree constructed from RAC trace entries.

---

## O

### **Operator**
A deterministic RUAX function that transforms system state.
