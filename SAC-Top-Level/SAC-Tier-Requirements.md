# Safety Asset Class (SAC) — Tier Requirements
## Foundation for Aligned Intelligence, Truth, and Humanity (FAITH)

The Safety Asset Class (SAC) defines three certification tiers that represent increasing levels of determinism, legality, auditability, and hardware‑enforced safety.  
Each tier builds on the one below it, forming a vertically integrated safety stack.

---

# 1. Purpose of SAC Tiers

SAC tiers ensure:

- progressive safety guarantees,
- deterministic system behavior,
- constitutional constraint preservation,
- legal compliance under MVL,
- immutable auditability under RAC,
- hardware‑level enforcement under CSL.

Each tier adds new guarantees and new enforcement mechanisms.

---

# 2. Tier 1 — Software Determinism

Tier 1 certifies that a system satisfies:

### 2.1 Deterministic Refinement (R³)
- deterministic refinement steps,
- fixed‑point convergence,
- constraint‑preserving updates.

### 2.2 Deterministic Execution (RUAX)
- deterministic operator execution,
- bounded‑error arithmetic,
- constraint‑safe composition.

### 2.3 Constraint Preservation
\[
C(S) = \text{true} \quad \forall S
\]

### 2.4 Required Subsystems
- **[R³ Refinement Rules](ca://s?q=Generate_R3_Refinement_Rules)**  
- **[RUAX Execution Rules](ca://s?q=Generate_RUAX_Execution_Rules)**  

### 2.5 Guarantees
- deterministic behavior,
- constraint‑safe execution,
- refinement‑safe evolution.

---

# 3. Tier 2 — Legal & Audit Integration

Tier 2 adds legality and auditability.

### 3.1 Legal Compliance (MVL)
- deterministic legal evaluation,
- legal constraint preservation,
- legal replayability.

### 3.2 Immutable Auditability (RAC)
- deterministic replay,
- immutable trace anchoring,
- Merkle‑verified block structure.

### 3.3 Cross‑Layer Verification
Tier 2 systems must satisfy:
\[
C(S) = \text{true} \quad \land \quad L(S) = \text{true}
\]

### 3.4 Required Subsystems
- **[MVL Enforcement Rules](ca://s?q=Generate_MVL_Enforcement_Rules)**  
- **[RAC Trace Format](ca://s?q=Generate_RAC_Trace_Format)**  
- **[RAC Enforcement Rules](ca://s?q=Generate_RAC_Enforcement_Rules)**  

### 3.5 Guarantees
- legal reproducibility,
- immutable auditability,
- deterministic replay.
