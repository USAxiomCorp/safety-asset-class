# Safety Asset Class (SAC) — Enforcement Rules
## Foundation for Aligned Intelligence, Truth, and Humanity (FAITH)

SAC Enforcement Rules define the unified enforcement framework across all layers of the Safety Asset Class.  
These rules ensure that constitutional constraints, legal constraints, deterministic execution, immutable auditability, and hardware‑level safety are enforced consistently and without exception.

SAC enforcement is the highest authority governing aligned intelligence.

---

# 1. Purpose of SAC Enforcement

SAC enforcement ensures:

- deterministic system behavior,
- constitutional constraint preservation,
- legal compliance under MVL,
- refinement‑safe evolution under R³,
- immutable auditability under RAC,
- hardware‑level enforcement under CSL.

SAC enforcement is absolute and non‑bypassable.

---

# 2. Enforcement Architecture

SAC enforcement spans **five layers**, each enforcing a different aspect of safety:

1. **R³ — Refinement Enforcement**  
2. **MVL — Legal Enforcement**  
3. **RUAX — Execution Enforcement**  
4. **RAC — Audit Enforcement**  
5. **CSL — Hardware Enforcement**  

Each layer enforces the layer below it.
