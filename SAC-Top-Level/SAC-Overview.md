# Safety Asset Class (SAC) — Overview
## Foundation for Aligned Intelligence, Truth, and Humanity (FAITH)

The Safety Asset Class (SAC) is the unified, constitutional, deterministic, and hardware‑enforced safety standard for aligned intelligence.  
SAC defines the complete safety substrate across refinement, legality, execution, auditability, and hardware enforcement.

SAC is the top‑level standard governing all aligned intelligent systems.

---

# 1. Purpose of SAC

SAC ensures:

- deterministic system behavior,
- constitutional constraint preservation,
- legal compliance under MVL,
- refinement‑safe evolution,
- immutable auditability,
- hardware‑level enforcement,
- zero‑violation guarantees.

SAC is the global safety standard for aligned intelligence.

# 2. SAC Architecture

SAC consists of **five subsystems**, each responsible for a different layer of safety:

1. **R³ — Refinement Rules & Fixed‑Point Safety**  
2. **MVL — Machine‑Verifiable Law**  
3. **RUAX — Deterministic Execution Model**  
4. **RAC — Immutable Audit Chain**  
5. **CSL — Constitutional Safety Layer (Hardware)**  

These subsystems form a vertically integrated safety stack.
